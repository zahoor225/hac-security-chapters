Chapter Guide

What is a Chapter?

A chapter of an organisation is like a smaller part of the organisation working under umbrella like parent organisation. Chapters, however being dependent on the parent organisations have more or less say in how activities are handled in each individual chapter while following the common rules established.

Purpose of our Chapters :

• HAC-SEC Chapters exist to make people aware of the security flaws in the world of internet and finding ways to reduce them and in the first place not let them originate.
• The HAC-SEC community encourages people with the interest of how things work in the internet world and getting involved with the local HAC-SEC Chapter.
• The Chapters can help people connect to the main organisation and work in spreading awareness while also connecting to people locally.
• The chapters enable people to hold meetings, contribute to projects, and organise training and events.
